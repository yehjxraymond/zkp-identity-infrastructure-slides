## zk-SNARK

<Steps>

- <Highlighted>Zero-knowledge</Highlighted> - Does not reveal information beyond validity of statement itself
- <Highlighted>Succinct</Highlighted> - Proofs are small and verified quickly, even for huge programs
- <Highlighted>Non-interactive</Highlighted> - Proof is a single message sent from prover to verifier
- <Highlighted>Argument of Knowledge</Highlighted> - Argument that prover knows of certain secrets 

</Steps>
